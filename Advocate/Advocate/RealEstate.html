Introduction

Real estate law is a specialized area of legal practice that encompasses various aspects related to the ownership, use, and transfer of real property. Here are some key aspects of real estate law:

Property Ownership: Defines the legal rights and responsibilities associated with owning real estate, including land, buildings, and natural resources.

Real Estate Transactions: Governs the process of buying, selling, leasing, or renting real property, ensuring agreements are legally binding and properly executed.

Contracts: Involves drafting and interpreting contracts related to real estate transactions, including purchase agreements, leases, and financing arrangements.
